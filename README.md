# Honesty Matrix

*by Norbert Nopper*

### When you tell the truth, do you know or believe it?

## The Four States of Honesty

|           | Consciously                   | Unconsciously                                   |
|-----------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| **Truth** | Consciously telling the truth | Unconsciously telling the truth (Freudian slip) |
| **Lie**   | Deliberately lying            | Unconsciously lying (White lie) \*\*            |

### Explanation

- Consciously telling the truth - One wants to tell the truth on purpose.
- Deliberately lying - One does not want to tell the truth on purpose.  
- Unconsciously telling the truth - One is not aware of telling the truth \*.
- Unconsciously lying - One is not aware of the untruth being told \*\*.

\* Reinterpretation: A Freudian slip reveals an unconscious wish — here reinterpreted as unconscious truth.

\*\* Reinterpretation: A white lie is a minor lie which could be considered harmless, or even beneficial, in the long term — here reinterpreted as unconscious lying, because one cannot know its true long-term impact.
